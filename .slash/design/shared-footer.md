# Shared Footer

## Architecture
A structured, multi-column footer to ground the page and provide secondary navigation and legal links.

## Visuals
- **Background**: `--bg-tertiary` or `--bg-deep` to differentiate from the main canvas (`--bg-primary`).
- **Padding**: 64px 24px (Top/Bottom, Left/Right)
- **Border**: 1px solid `--border-primary` on the top edge.

## Layout (Desktop)
- **Column 1 (Left)**: Logo + Short description ("AI 기반 로고 디자인 스튜디오") + Copyright © 2026 Splash.
- **Column 2**: 제품 (작동 방식, 기능, 요금제)
- **Column 3**: 회사 (소개, 블로그, 연락처)
- **Column 4**: 지원 (FAQ, 이용약관, 개인정보처리방침)

## Layout (Mobile)
- Stacked columns. Left column goes to the bottom or top, followed by expanding/collapsing lists or simply stacked links.

## Typography
- Headers: 14px, semibold, `--text-primary`
- Links: 14px, `--text-secondary`, hover: `--text-primary`
